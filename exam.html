Machine Learning Project


Backround

In this project, the data is collected by accelerometers on the belt , forearm, arm and dumbbell of 6 participants. They were asked to perform barbell lifts correctly and incorrectly in 5 different ways. The target of this project is to predict the manner of execution

Method

First I choose the variables without a Nan as training Data.
For testing I use the same variables.
For predicting I decided to use a tree classification like Random Forest or LDA. It turns out that LDA is the most accurate algorithm.
So I used LDA to predicting the outcome.
